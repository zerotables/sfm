About Us

SoulfulMeal is more than food—it’s a journey of purity, taste, and wellbeing. We are a cloud kitchen by ZEROTABLES FOOD TECH LLP, based in Bangalore, dedicated to serving pure vegetarian (No Onion, No Garlic) food that nourishes both body and mind.

Our philosophy is simple: food should be sattvik, wholesome, and full of flavor. Whether you’re seeking a balance of nutrition, honoring tradition, or simply indulging in clean, delicious meals—you’ll find SoulfulMeal to be a destination you can trust.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Sattvik Commitment: We strictly follow the practice of cooking without onion and garlic, embracing a purer, more mindful culinary path.
  • Quality You Can Taste: Only fresh, high-quality ingredients. Every recipe is crafted with care to bring out natural flavors without compromising on health.
  • Versatility & Flexibility: From hearty breakfasts, à la carte meals, to large-scale catering—we cater to all your dining needs.
  • Rooted in Culture, Tuned for Today: Drawing inspiration from tradition while keeping pace with modern tastes—SoulfulMeal makes sattvik food accessible, appealing, and relevant.
  • Convenience: As a cloud kitchen, we streamline operations so you get fresh, clean food delivered to your doorstep—or served at your events—with minimal fuss.

Our Mission

At SoulfulMeal, our mission is to inspire healthier living through sattvik cuisine. We believe that eating pure is not a restriction—it’s a path to more mindful, joyful eating. We aim to be your trusted partner in every meal that uplifts your spirit, supports your wellness, and honors your values.

Our Vision

To become Bangalore’s leading name in sattvik food—where every dish we deliver is a blend of purity, taste, and satisfaction, contributing to a healthier, happier community.
